Member,Board of Directors

Thareendra Kalpage, born on June 26, 1981, is a Sri Lankan-Asian entrepreneur and co-founder of Digital Zyndicate Co., a venture capital firm in Denver, Colorado, and Meetanu, a software firm in the hospitality industry. He's also invested in Duthaya Digital Services, which operates the Duthaya mobile app, connecting freelance labor with local demand. He co-owns a premium leather bag range with a leading US retailer. With over 20 years of experience, he served as COO and later CEO of Epic Lanka Technologies, a prominent software firm in Sri Lanka. He expanded Epic Lanka into new sectors, collaborating on projects like South East Asia's First Ever Mobile Lottery App and launching HelaViru Digital Market Place with Sri Lanka Telecom. Prior to Epic Lanka, he worked at Microsoft Sri Lanka for over 5 years, achieving significant milestones in government engagement and technology adoption. He also contributed to the formation of Samson Information Technologies. Mr. Kalpage is known for his writings on economics, technology, and global politics. He actively supports young founders, provides strategic advice, and mentors leadership teams. He holds an LLB (Hons) in Law, attended the Senior Management Programme at Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy, and is certified in Big Data and Cloud Computing Technologies from Yonsei University.
